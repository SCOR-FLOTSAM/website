Working Group meetings

The first meeting of the SCOR-FLOTSAM Working Group was on 11 March 2018 at the Hilton Mission Bay in San Diego, California, USA, on the Sunday before the 6th International Marine Debris Conference.

The second meeting will be in the week of 6-10 May 2019, in Utrecht, Netherlands. More info to follow in due time.

The third and final meeting will then be in Summer or early Autumn of 2020, location to be decided.

Other meetings

Other relevant meetings where members of the Working Group will present their work include:

  • EGU – April 2018, Vienna
  • MICRO2018 – Nov 2018, Lanzarote
  • EGU – April 2019, Vienna
  • IUGG – July 2019, Montreal
  • OceanObs – September 2019, Honolulu
